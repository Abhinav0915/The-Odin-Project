Pasta


Description

Ingredients

  • 225 gm pasta penne
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 teaspoon basil
  • salt as required
  • 2 pinches powdered black pepper
  • 2 red chilli
  • 450 gm roma tomato
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 tablespoon parsley
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cup water

Steps

  1. Prepare the red sauce and boil the pasta
  2. Cook tomatoes along with herbs for 10-12 minutes
  3. Pasta is ready to relish
  4. Serve
